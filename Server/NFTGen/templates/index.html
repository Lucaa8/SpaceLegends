Space Legends

Space Legends is a 2D platformer game where players complete various artifacts by progressing through the game. Players can also buy and sell items on the Galactic Market to advance more quickly. The in-game currency (StarDust) and items (Cosmic Relics) are deployed on the blockchain, making them immutable and fully owned by the players.
